About ShaadiGuide

Planning guidance with cultural care

Learn about ShaadiGuide’s mission, readers, editorial standards and respectful approach to regional and family differences.

Our mission

ShaadiGuide helps couples and families plan Indian weddings with more clarity and less pressure. We bring practical organisation together with respectful explanations of Hindu wedding culture.

Who we serve

Our guides are for couples, parents, relatives, friends and guests in India and around the world. We write for people continuing family traditions, adapting them, learning about a partner’s culture or creating a celebration that combines several backgrounds.

Our editorial standards

We aim for useful, original and clearly structured content. We separate practical suggestions from cultural or religious explanation, avoid presenting one practice as universal and do not invent auspicious dates or claim spiritual authority.

Sources and verification

We use primary or authoritative sources when a claim can be verified, especially for legal and government procedures. Cultural guides also rely on careful comparison and clearly identify practices that differ; family-specific or religious details should be checked locally.

Respect for difference

Indian wedding traditions differ by region, language, community, family and individual values. Where religious detail matters, we encourage readers to speak with their priest, pandit, officiant or elders.

Updates and corrections

We revisit pages when procedures, features or reliable information change. Readers can report a possible error through the contact page; meaningful corrections are made at the source so regenerated pages remain consistent.

Practical and original content

Our checklists, wording examples and planning tools are created for ShaadiGuide. They are starting points to adapt—not substitutes for personal, legal, financial or religious advice.
