Planning an interregional wedding

Ask each partner to identify the rites that are essential, meaningful, optional or unsuitable. Compare the lists with both officiants before booking a ceremony duration. Use accurate names in the invitation and programme, and explain key transitions without presenting either family as the default.

Helpful answers

Frequently asked questions

How should I use a regional wedding guide?

Use it to learn common terms and prepare questions. Confirm the actual sequence with family elders and the officiant.

Can two regional traditions be combined?

Yes, when both partners and families agree. Keep distinct religious rites clear and let officiants advise on sequence.

Are ceremony names consistent across India?

No. Similar actions may have different names, and the same name may describe different practices.

How do we brief vendors?

Give vendors the approved run sheet, pronunciation notes, family roles and photography boundaries rather than asking them to supply a generic tradition list.
