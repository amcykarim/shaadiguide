Helpful answers

Frequently asked questions

Are these answers rules for every Indian wedding?

No. They are practical starting points. Family customs, ceremony needs, contracts, locations and guest circumstances vary.

Do the guides include vendor prices?

No. Use current written quotations and the browser-based calculators to compare your own scenarios.

Where should a first-time planner begin?

Start with the Getting Started guides, then move to budget, guests, ceremonies and vendors in that order when it suits your family.

Can the checklists replace a planner or officiant?

No. They organise decisions; they do not replace specialist, contractual or religious guidance.
