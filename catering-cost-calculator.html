Helpful planning tips

  • Confirm the minimum guaranteed covers and final-count deadline.
  • Ask how children, vendor meals and staff meals are priced.
  • Check whether crockery, servers, water, gas and clean-up are included.

Common cost mistakes

  • Applying a guest buffer twice.
  • Forgetting service charge is sometimes taxed.
  • Assuming every event has the same guest count.

Frequently asked questions

Is the catering cost result an exact quotation?

No. It is a planning estimate based only on the figures entered. Vendor inclusions, taxes, timing and contract terms may change the final cost.

Does this calculator save my information?

No. The calculation runs in your browser and does not submit personal information or require registration.

Can I print the result?

Yes. Use the Print Summary button to produce a clean page showing the entered figures, results, breakdown and disclaimer.

How should blank optional fields be handled?

Leave them blank or enter zero. The calculator treats blank optional amounts as zero.
