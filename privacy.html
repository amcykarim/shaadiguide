Privacy Policy

Read the general privacy policy for ShaadiGuide, a static informational website, including cookies, analytics, advertising and user choices.

Last updated: 20 July 2026

ShaadiGuide is currently a static informational website. This policy explains what may be processed when you visit and how we will communicate future changes.

Log information

Our web host may automatically process standard technical logs such as IP address, browser type, requested page, date and time, and referring page for security and reliable delivery. ShaadiGuide does not operate a user database, account system, backend form handler or document-upload service. Calculators, generators, local search and checklist progress run in the browser; current checklists do not use localStorage.

Cookies

We do not currently set advertising, account or personalisation cookies. Essential hosting infrastructure may use limited technical mechanisms. If we add optional cookies, we will update this notice and provide choices where required.

Advertising

Pages contain labelled advertising placeholders only. No advertising network is currently installed. If advertising is introduced, partners may process information under their own policies and applicable consent requirements.

Analytics

ShaadiGuide has not installed an analytics service on this site. If analytics is added, we will describe the provider, purpose and available controls.

External links

Links to external sites are governed by those sites’ privacy practices. Review them before providing information.

Children’s privacy

This site is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children through accounts or forms.

Your choices

You may control browser settings, decline to email us and leave the site at any time. Contact us to ask about information you have voluntarily sent by email.

Contact and updates

Questions may be sent to hello@shaadiguide.in. We may update this policy when the site’s features or legal requirements change; the date above will show the latest revision.
