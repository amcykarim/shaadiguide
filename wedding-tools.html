Helpful answers

Frequently asked questions

Do the tools use live vendor prices?

No. Every calculator uses only the values you enter. Ask vendors for current written quotations.

Is information sent anywhere?

No. The tools run locally in your browser and do not require an account.

Can families print the estimates?

Yes. Each tool includes a print-friendly summary for planning discussions.

Are these financial recommendations?

No. They are organisational estimates, not financial, tax or legal advice.
