Disclaimer

Important disclaimers about ShaadiGuide’s general information, cultural variation, budget estimates, vendors and external links.

Educational purpose

ShaadiGuide content is provided for general education and wedding planning. Although we aim to be careful and useful, information may be incomplete, become outdated or not fit your circumstances.

Cultural and religious variation

Indian and Hindu wedding customs vary by region, community, lineage and family. Descriptions are introductions, not religious rulings. Confirm ceremony details with your priest, pandit, officiant or elders.

Budget calculator estimates

The calculators apply arithmetic to figures entered by the visitor. They do not account for every tax, fee, contract term, price change or personal circumstance. Results are estimates and not quotations or promises of cost.

Legal and administrative information

Marriage-registration and document-planning pages summarise general questions to verify. Official requirements can differ by law, jurisdiction and authority; always use the linked government service and seek qualified advice when needed.

Vendors and purchases

Any vendor-related guidance is general. Check credentials, contracts, insurance where relevant, product details, quotations and independent references before committing.

No professional advice

Nothing on this site is legal, financial, medical, religious or other professional advice.

External links

We are not responsible for the accuracy, availability, security or policies of external websites. A link does not necessarily imply endorsement; confirm important instructions at their official source.
