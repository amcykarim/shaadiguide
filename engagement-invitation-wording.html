How to make the wording your own

  • Choose hosting language that reflects who is genuinely inviting.
  • Keep practical details separate and easy to scan.
  • Use religious titles only after family or officiant review.
  • State restrictions politely and consistently across card, message and RSVP.
  • Test every digital link before sending.

Helpful answers

Frequently asked questions

May I adapt these examples?

Yes. Replace every placeholder and adjust the hosting language, tone and event details for your family.

What should I proofread?

Check names, relationships, date, weekday, time, venue, city, RSVP method and any links with at least two people.

Can I use this wording in another language?

Use a fluent writer or translator and have the final text proofread. The English examples are guidance, not exact translations.
