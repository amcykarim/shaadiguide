Invitation-writing checklist

  • Confirm the spelling and preferred order of every name.
  • Name the exact event and who is invited to it.
  • Check date, weekday, time, time zone, venue and city.
  • Use one clear RSVP method and realistic deadline.
  • Proofread cultural and religious titles with both families.
  • Verify map, accommodation and livestream placeholders before sending.

Helpful answers

Frequently asked questions

What details should every invitation include?

Include the couple or honourees, event name, date, start time, venue, city and a clear RSVP method. Add dress, travel or ceremony notes only when useful.

Can I copy these examples?

Yes. Adapt names, hosts, dates, event details and tone. Proofread every customised version before sending or printing.

How should bilingual wording be prepared?

Ask a fluent reader or professional translator to write and proof the second language. Do not rely on transliteration alone for religious or family titles.

What should a WhatsApp invitation include?

Keep the key details readable, verify any map link and avoid sending private livestream or contact information to unintended groups.
